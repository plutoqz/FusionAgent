# Thesis Related-Work Matrix

This writing-facing matrix is derived from `2026-05-06-related-work-gap-matrix.*` and keeps every comparison tied to the same thesis main line: a KG-grounded, contract-bounded, executable geospatial fusion runtime with auditable evidence.

## Geo-Agent

- Closest overlap: natural-language GIS interaction and spatial intent decomposition.
- Our difference: FusionAgent ends in executable fusion artifacts plus audit evidence instead of interactive GIS outputs alone.
- Borrowed idea: spatial intent decomposition and graph-aware retrieval.
- Non-comparable boundary: do not position FusionAgent as a general GIS copilot.

## CyVerACT

- Closest overlap: schema-aware deterministic verification and structured error reasoning.
- Our difference: FusionAgent couples verification with executable planning, healing, and artifact evidence.
- Borrowed idea: machine-readable error metadata and deterministic verification loops.
- Non-comparable boundary: do not reduce the thesis to text-to-query correctness.

## OntoLLM

- Closest overlap: ontology-grounded reasoning constraints and digression prevention.
- Our difference: FusionAgent binds KG structure directly to executable tasks, algorithms, and outputs.
- Borrowed idea: ontology-grounded rejection and bounded reasoning discipline.
- Non-comparable boundary: do not expand into a broad ontology-engineering agenda.

## PathMind

- Closest overlap: path prioritization and low-noise retrieval.
- Our difference: FusionAgent adds bounded execution, healing, and evidence writeback after retrieval.
- Borrowed idea: path prioritization and token-efficiency heuristics.
- Non-comparable boundary: do not add multi-round self-dialogue just to imitate path-search literature.

## UniAI-GraphRAG

- Closest overlap: dual-channel retrieval and ontology-guided extraction.
- Our difference: FusionAgent is about executable geospatial fusion outputs, not text QA or report synthesis endpoints.
- Borrowed idea: dual-channel retrieval summaries and structured extraction patterns.
- Non-comparable boundary: do not reposition the runtime as a generic GraphRAG system.

## 地下车站洪水脆弱性级联效应分析

- Closest overlap: facility dependency and flood cascade reasoning in disaster scenarios.
- Our difference: FusionAgent contributes a bounded scenario runtime and evidence contract instead of a domain-specific simulation platform.
- Borrowed idea: bounded facility dependency and cascade reasoning for scenario profiles.
- Non-comparable boundary: do not turn the scenario layer into a full digital-twin thesis.

## Writing Rule

For thesis prose, use these comparisons to answer four questions consistently:

1. where the closest overlap really is
2. where FusionAgent is categorically different
3. what is worth borrowing
4. which tempting analogy must be rejected because it would overstate the runtime boundary
